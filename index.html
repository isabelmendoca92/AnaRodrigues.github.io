Ana Rodrigues
PhD in Computer Science (Specialization in Human–Computer Interaction)

I believe that technology plays a crucial role in shaping a better society. My research adopts a human-centered perspective, carefully considering the influence and impact that technological systems have on people. In particular, I study how interactive systems can support human creativity and decision-making in AI-mediated environments, preserving human agency.

To date, my research has focused on three main areas:

  • Creativity Support Tools (CSTs)
    I investigate the design of interactive systems that support human creativity.
    In particular, I explore how AI technologies, such as large language models, can assist with idea generation, evaluation, and selection while maintaining human control and reflective thinking.
  • Human-AI Interaction
    I study how people interact with AI systems and how interfaces can support meaningful collaboration between humans and artificial intelligence.
    My research examines how design choices influence trust, understanding, and human agency when working with AI systems.
  • Decision-making in AI-mediated environments
    My current research investigates how AI systems influence human decision-making and judgment.
    I explore how interactive systems can be designed to support reflective decision-making rather than simply providing automated recommendations, contributing to human flourishing in AI-supported workflows.
