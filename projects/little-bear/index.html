Designing an Emotional Storytelling Experience for Children

A collaborative 3D animation project exploring how visual simplicity, character design, and environment choices can support emotional engagement for children aged 4–7.

Overview

This project was developed as part of a 3D animation course, where we created a short story about a little bear trying to make and keep a friend.

I contributed to the background design, the snowman development, and the early concept research phase.
